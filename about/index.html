IEEE CEAL Student Branch

The IEEE Student Branch at College of Engineering Attingal (CEAL) is a dynamic community of students passionate about technology, innovation, and collaboration. Affiliated with the world's largest technical professional organization, our branch provides a platform for aspiring engineers to explore emerging fields in electrical, electronics, and computer sciences through hands-on projects, workshops, and technical events.

  • Active since 2018 with 100+ student members across various disciplines.
  • Recognized for organizing impactful events like the Student Development Program and TechTalk series.
  • Strong presence in IEEE Kerala Section with participation in flagship events like IEEE LINK and All Kerala SB Congress.
  • Collaborations with societies such as IEEE Computer Society, PES, and WIE Affinity Group.
  • Focus on technical growth, community service, and professional networking for students.

IEEE Signal Processing Society (SPS)

The IEEE Signal Processing Society (SPS) is a professional society within the IEEE community. SPS is dedicated to advancing signal processing theory as well as practical applications. Signal processing is the process of evaluating, modifying and extracting information from a variety of signals, such as audio, pictures, video, and other data. The IEEE SPS fosters the advancement of signal processing theory, algorithms, and technology, providing a collaborative forum for researchers, engineers, and professionals in the field. It's a hub for sharing knowledge, keeping up with the newest advances, and supporting creativity in the field of signal processing.

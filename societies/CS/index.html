IEEE Computer Society (CS)

The IEEE Computer Society is one of the world's most prominent sources of technical information, community services, and personalized services for computing and computer science professionals. IEEE Computer Society strives to be integral to the global technical community and computer professionals everywhere, as well as recognized worldwide for technical professionals' contributions to research, development, and use of technology to enhance global conditions.

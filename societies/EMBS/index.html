IEEE Engineering in Medicine and Biology Society (EMBS)

The IEEE Engineering in Medicine and Biology Society (EMBS) is the world's largest international society of biomedical engineers. EMBS members are focused on the development and application of engineering concepts and methods to provide new solutions to biological, medical and healthcare problems.

IEEE FISAT Student Branch

The Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers (IEEE) stands as the premier global organization dedicated to fostering technological innovation and excellence for the benefit of humanity. With over 400,000 members in more than 160 countries, IEEE is the world's largest technical professional organization, committed to advancing the fields of electrical engineering, computer science, and related disciplines through its prolific activities and extensive network.
